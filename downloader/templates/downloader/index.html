Frequently Asked Questions

How can I download a full YouTube channel?

Simply copy the URL of the YouTube channel (e.g. https://www.youtube.com/@channelname) and paste it into the search bar. The app will quickly retrieve the videos list, let you choose which videos to download, select your format, and batch download them in parallel, zipping them together for a one-click download.

What is the maximum download quality?

For MP3 (audio), you can choose quality presets up to 320kbps for premium audio. For MP4 (video), it downloads the best quality stream available (up to 1080p and higher) and merges the audio and video automatically.

Where are downloaded files saved?

If you run this application locally, files are saved directly inside the media/downloads/ directory in the project folder. You can also download them directly to your browser's Downloads folder from the web UI.
